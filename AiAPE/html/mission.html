Mission

Our Vision and Mission

Vision

We have started various training courses all over India with the help and cooperation of educational experts. Our goal is to train unemployed educated boys and girls, aiming to be recognized as a new model of institution. We seek far-reaching engagement with the business community and international partners, shaping the future success of our students in the global environment.

Mission

To achieve excellent standards of quality skills-based education by keeping pace with rapidly changing technologies and creating technical manpower of global standards capable of accepting new challenges.

Our Efforts

Our efforts are dedicated to imparting quality and value-based education, raising the satisfaction level of all stakeholders. Our strength is directed towards creating competent professionals, and our endeavor is to provide all possible support to promote All India Institute of Nursery Teacher Training & Education (AIINTTE) activities.

Commitment to Quality Education

We are committed to delivering quality education that meets the needs of the profession and society, achieving excellence in skills-based training and education.

Professional Ethics

We practice and promote high standards of professional ethics, transparency, and accountability.

Academy’s Mission

The Academy’s mission is to re-engage youth in the active pursuit of self-improvement, interpersonal growth, and educational development, putting them on a path to success. This mission will be served by providing students with an individualized and self-paced program, set within a flexible scheduling environment responsive to their individual needs, maximizing each student’s ability to earn skills-based training and education.

About AIINTTE

All India Institute of Nursery Teacher Training & Education (AIINTTE) is a distinguished government-registered institute (under the Trust Registration Act) located in Delhi, India. We conduct a variety of vocational and skills-based courses, as well as certificate and diploma programs. To date, we have trained thousands of teaching professionals.
