Our History

Founded in the early 1990s, AIAPE started as a small initiative with a vision to provide quality education and holistic development to students. Over the years, the institution has grown into a leading educational establishment with a commitment to excellence. Our history is marked by numerous milestones, including the expansion of our facilities, introduction of innovative programs, and significant contributions to the community. Today, AIAPE stands as a beacon of educational success, driven by a mission to foster learning and personal growth.

1990 - Founding

AIAPE was established with the goal of providing exceptional education and creating a nurturing environment for students. The founding team, led by visionaries in education, set the stage for a new era in learning.

2000 - Facility Expansion

In the early 2000s, the institution underwent significant expansion, adding new classrooms, laboratories, and recreational facilities. This growth allowed AIAPE to accommodate more students and offer a wider range of programs.

2010 - Innovative Programs

AIAPE introduced a series of innovative programs designed to enhance student engagement and learning. These programs included new extracurricular activities, advanced technology integration, and partnerships with leading organizations.

2020 - Community Outreach

Our commitment to community involvement grew stronger with the launch of several outreach initiatives. These programs focused on educational support, social responsibility, and fostering stronger ties with the local community.

2024 - Current Achievements

AIAPE continues to excel in its mission, achieving numerous accolades for academic excellence and innovation. The institution remains dedicated to providing top-quality education and preparing students for a successful future.
